Chidera · Sister

My partner in crime

Happy birthday Ifeomachukwu. The person who knows all my embarrassing stories but still secretly brags about me, sometimes though to everyone.

You’re among the first people I call when something good happens, and also when I want to gossip about someone because you seem to always have my back.

Thank you for all the little things that add up: for sharing your clothes even when I stretched them out, for hyping me up, and for being the partner in crime and supporter when it comes to our parents and other people.

I used to wish that I had a little sister to be my mini me because we liked different things growing up, but I have realized that I do not need another sister. I am okay with having just one sister because I know that we will always have each other’s back.

I hope this year brings you everything you’ve been working for and some surprises you didn’t even know you wanted. HAPPY BIRTHDAY ONCE MORE.
